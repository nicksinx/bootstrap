# Summary

Perplexity Spaces are dedicated workspaces for organizing research, threads, and files. Spaces can apply custom instructions to every interaction, support collaboration, and let users pin or search files and threads. Pro users can upload up to 50 files per Space; files uploaded to threads may count toward weekly upload allowances rather than Space limits.

# Relevant claims

- Spaces are dedicated workspaces for organizing research and collaboration.
- Spaces can apply custom AI instructions to every interaction in that Space.
- Pro subscribers can upload up to 50 files per Space.
- Files uploaded to threads count toward weekly upload allowances rather than Space counts.
- Enterprise users can use connectors such as Google Drive, Dropbox, Box, OneDrive, and SharePoint.

# Limitations and uncertainty

Help center documents Spaces rather than a separate “Desktop Pro Projects” spec. Treating Spaces as the verified Perplexity research surface for OKF is an inference supported by docs, not an explicit multi-tool integration policy.
